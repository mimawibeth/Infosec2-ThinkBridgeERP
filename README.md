# ThinkBridge ERP

ThinkBridge is a centralized platform designed for research, development, and knowledge management. It streamlines project workflows, enhances team collaboration, and secures organizational data — all within a single, unified system.

## Capabilities

- **Project & Task Management** – Plan, assign, and track work across teams
- **Team Collaboration** – Centralized activity feeds, knowledge base, and task discussions
- **Calendar & Scheduling** – Shared calendar for events and milestones
- **Product Lifecycle Management** – Monitor products through every development stage
- **Reports & Analytics** – Gain insights into projects, tasks, and team performance
- **Role-Based Access Control** – Granular permissions for Super Admin, Company Admin, Project Manager, and Team Member
- **Subscription & Billing** – Plan management backed by secure payment processing
- **Theme Support** – Light and dark mode for user preference
